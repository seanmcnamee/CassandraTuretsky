Inspiration

Cassie's art often relates to her experiences with mental illness. Her personal experiences with depression, anxiety, panic and mood disorders and OCD have led to her melancholy and raw portfolio of works, ranging from drawing and painting to photogrpahy and printmaking.

Cassie is also often influenced by film and media. Moody cinematography as well as the photgrpahy of artists like Alex Prager and Nan Goldin are major inspirations for Cassandra.
