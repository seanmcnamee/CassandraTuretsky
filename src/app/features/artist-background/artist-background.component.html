Artist Background

In her adolescant years Cassie attended Connetquot High school in Bohemia, where she took advantage of nearly every art class they had to offer. While her focus at the time was naturally on drawing, she took interest in other mediums including photography, film and pottery. During her last two years at Connetquot, all of her free periods were spent at the pottery wheel or sitting in on film lectures and viewings. In these same years, Cassie became a very active member of National Art Honor Society.

The most important experience Cassie had as a young artist was meeting and learning from her high school art teacher for three years, John Rapone. Rapone fostered his student's passion for art and pushed her to succeed in the future by challenging her more and more every day, while just as well being a face of kindness during times of struggle with mental illness up until her graduation from the school in 2018.

In 2021, Cassie received her Associates Degree from the Fashion Institute of Technology in New York City. Currently, she is going into her fourth year at the school and will be receiving her Bachelors in Fine Art next spring.
